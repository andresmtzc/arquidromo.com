Interiors PR.

 

In The White Room has a dedicated team of lifestyle and home interiors PR experts with excellent blogger, influencer, online publication and print media contacts.

Our PR campaigns have generated valuable sales enquiries in addition to building significant brand awareness. Above all, we have a real passion for the home interest sector and a genuine understanding of how the media works and how the right print and online exposure can help a brand grow.

PR Campaigns.

Every brand, product and person has a story. Our team of PR specialists will work with you to launch a bespoke campaign that delivers your brand messages to your target media audiences. 

We spend time building great relationships with the most influential editors, journalists, bloggers and online influencers in the industry to make sure we achieve great results and generate valuable sales enquiries for each of our clients. From consumer publications and national newspapers to blogs and online titles, as well as the trade and design press, our extensive media database covers a wide variety of sectors enabling us to get your message to the correct audience.

 
 

Online PR.

 

Our experienced team of lifestyle and home interiors experts delivers excellent results through tailored online PR campaigns. We will work closely with you to achieve your company’s digital goals, reach your target audiences and raise significant brand awareness. From securing coverage on national websites, providing influential blogs with editorial content and generating quality backlinks to your website, our online activity forms a core part of all of our ongoing PR campaigns.   

 
 
 
 
 

Trade PR.

From the kitchen and bathroom trade, to design and architecture press, our PR campaigns are tailored to suit your target audiences. With years of experience working with the B2B media, we understand how to effectively communicate messages and news stories to the trade and have built up a portfolio of valuable media relationships within a variety of sectors, including yachting, hospitality, interior design, architecture, painting & decorating and flooring. 

 
 

Working with Influencers.

Love them or hate them, influencers can support your brand. We’re firm believers in influencer marketing as long as it’s carried out in a way that will genuinely benefit a client. Whether gifting product or paying for dedicated content, we have seen the positive impact the right kind of influencer collaboration can have on a brand, from growth in brand awareness and online visibility to increased web traffic and sales. 

Our interiors PR experts have built up relationships with some of the most influential bloggers and influencers across a broad range of sectors including lifestyle, interiors, design… and more! We’ve worked with a number of platforms and people to secure far-reaching coverage for our clients, creating valuable online exposure with the right audiences. 

 
 
 
 

Blog Writing.

From inspirational and topical blog posts to drafting SEO-friendly content for publication both on-site and off-site, we are committed to ensuring your brand is as discoverable as possible. Working alongside the SEO, digital and PR campaigns, our specialist in-house team of copywriters generates valuable blog content on a regular basis, helping to increase backlinks and improve Google rankings.
