It’s official, Cuba is this year’s cool kid on the block. And while it may be on the brink of a new era, there’s an energy and atmosphere right now in Cuba that just can’t be beat. Whether we like it or not, the changes in Cuba are happening RIGHT NOW, and with all that development going on, there’s never been a better time to experience Cuba’s fascinating mix of old and new. Let El Camino Travel introduce you to the movers and shakers behind Havana’s pulse: the artists, the musicians, the young entrepreneurs. Make new friends, get to know the city, dine with local families and immerse yourself in this warm and welcoming culture. If Cuba’s colorful colonial architecture, rum-filled cocktails, warm summer evenings, and postcard-perfect streets lined with vintage cars don’t get you dancing salsa on the street, then nothing else will!

We are Cuban travel experts and make it easy to have a trip of a lifetime. We stay on top of all the trends so you do not have to spend countless hours online doing research in a country you've never been to, all the while not knowing if you are getting sucked into major tourist traps (FYI there are MANY in Cuba). With us, you do not have to plan a single detail. You know as soon as you arrive, you'll be greeted by a local, who immediately feels like a friend. Someone who'll show you the best places to explore, to eat, drink and play! You'll experience every magical moment, with new friends and your own professional photographer taking care of all the photos. And at the end of your trip, you'll have memories no selfie stick could ever capture. That is the #elcaminotravel way.
